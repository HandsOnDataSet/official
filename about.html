About

The work contained in the project entitled “Data Science Demo” is a bonafide work of Subhajit Karmakar and Parmanand Kumar (Roll No. 111801041 and 111801030), carried out in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, Indian Institute of Technology Palakkad.
This project involves building an easy-to-use portable and demonstrable data science toolkit. The toolkit is able to do preprocessing of various datasets, visualization of results, storing datasets, data analytics, and various machine learning algorithms. Demos are available in Python, Jupyter notebook, and the Colab platform.
